# GHL SMS Messages

## Hot Lead - 90 Second SMS

Fires within 90 seconds of AI Tier being set to Hot.

```
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, saw your interest in our marketing services. I'm reaching out personally — what's the best time for a quick 15-minute call today?
```

## Missed Call Text Back

Fires automatically when a call is missed.

```
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, sorry we missed your call! We'll get back to you within 15 minutes. In the meantime, feel free to reply here with what you need help with.
```
